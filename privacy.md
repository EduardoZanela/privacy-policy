# Privacy Policy for NovaDriveLog

**Last Updated:** March 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NovaDriveLog ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Data Collection and Storage

NovaDriveLog is designed to be a "local-first" application. 

**Local Data:** All information you enter into the App, including vehicle details, fuel logs, expenses, and custom categories, is stored locally in a database on your device. We do not transmit, back up, or store this data on our servers. You have full control over your data.

## 2. Third-Party Services

While the core functionality of the App does not require transmitting your personal data to us, we use trusted third-party services to support and monetize the App. These services may collect certain technical and usage information:

### Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the App. AdMob may use device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID or Apple IDFA), cookies, and similar technologies to serve personalized ads based on your interests and usage patterns. 
- For more information on how Google uses data, please review [Google's Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).
- You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad tracking settings (e.g., "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your data is stored locally on your device, it is retained as long as you keep the App installed. 
- **Modifying Data:** You can edit or delete specific entries directly within the App.
- **Deleting All Data:** You can delete all your data by deleting the App from your device. Alternatively, if you use the "Clear and Replace" import function, it will overwrite your existing data.

*Note: Since we do not have access to your locally stored data, we cannot delete it for you remotely.*

## 4. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us, and we will take steps to ensure it is removed.

## 5. Security

We value the trust you place in us by using NovaDriveLog. While your data is protected by the security features of your device's operating system, please be aware that no security measure is perfect, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your device.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
